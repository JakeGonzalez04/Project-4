    Prep: 2 Minutes
    Cook: 6 Minutes
    Total: 8 Minutes
    Servings: 1 Serving
    Total Cost: $65.9

Ingredients

    Queso-Oaxaca:$30.00
    Corn-Tortillas:$16.95
    Choptle $17.77

Directions

  1. Shred the cheese to the amount of cheese you want in yout tortilla./li>
  2. Heat up each tortilla side in a skillet over medium heat for about minute.
  3. Once cheese is starting to melt close the tortilla into a half moon shape.
  4. Flip each side of the tortilla every minute for at least once or twice.
  5. Once cheese is melted place chipotle sauce inside the tortilla.
  6. Repeat process for as many quesadillas you want.

Notes

It may be expensive to buy all of the ingridients, but the ingridients will be able to make more than 20 quesadillas.
